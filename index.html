Whoopee
Comic Camera 

Whoopee is a new age comic creation camera app that turns your speech into animated speech bubbles.

WHOOPEE OVERVIEW

You can create a short video using the front and rear camera one after another without breaking the loop. What you say will convert into a comic speech bubble. You can add a variety of filters, stickers, emojis and much more in your creation. You can change the style and design of your speech bubble.
